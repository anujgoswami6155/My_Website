The history of chess dates back over 1,500 years...

Chess is a two-player strategy board game played on an 8Ã—8 checkered board with 64 squares. Each player controls 16 pieces: one king, one queen, two rooks, two knights, two bishops, and eight pawns. The objective of the game is to checkmate the opponent's king.

Important Chess Milestones:

  • World Chess Championship (1886): Wilhelm Steinitz became the first official World Chess Champion.
  • FIDE (1924): The world chess governing body was established.
  • Online Chess & AI (2000s-Present): Chess.com and Lichess have made chess more accessible, and AI engines like Stockfish and AlphaZero have revolutionized chess strategy.
